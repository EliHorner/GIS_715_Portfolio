Scale Lab

Map 1: Smaller Scale (and Grayscale)

Map 1 shows part of the Allentown/Bethlehem area of Pennsylvania. It focuses on the area near the river, specifically pointing out nearby transportation features, waterbodies, and political boundaries. The map is designed to show how these different features exist on the landscape and could be used by a visitor to see how to get around the area and some natural points of interest (if they like water).



Map 2: Larger Scale (and Color)

Map 2 shows the same area, but at a larger scale and with color. Notice in the bottom right, you can see the same areas shown in the first map. This map helps highlight some of the diffenrces we see in our transportation, waterbodies, and political boundaries when we zoom out.
